# NorthStar Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 13, 2024

---

## Overview

NorthStar ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

---

## Information Collection and Use

### What We Collect

NorthStar collects and stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

#### Health Data
- **Medication Information:** Names, dosages, schedules, and tracking of taken medications
- **Mood Logs:** Mood levels, energy levels, anxiety levels, and related notes
- **Sleep Data:** Sleep start/end times, quality ratings, interruptions, and notes
- **Side Effects:** Logged side effects associated with medications

#### HealthKit Data
- **Sleep Analysis:** The app can read sleep data from Apple HealthKit (with your explicit permission)
- This data is used only to automatically populate sleep tracking entries
- We never write data to HealthKit or modify your Health app data

#### Device Information
- **Local Notifications:** Medication reminder preferences and scheduled notifications
- **Widget Data:** Information shared with the home screen widget via App Groups

### What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ No personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No camera or microphone access
- ❌ No contacts or photos

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All health data is stored **locally on your device** using Core Data (Apple's encrypted database)
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the iCloud sync feature
- Data is protected by your device's passcode/Face ID/Touch ID

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Data is synced to **your personal iCloud account** using CloudKit
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Only accessible from devices signed in to your iCloud account
- We (the developers) cannot access your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

### App Groups
- The app uses App Groups to share data with the home screen widget
- This data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere

---

## Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

1. **Medication Management:** Display medication schedules, send reminders, track adherence
2. **Health Tracking:** Log and visualize mood and sleep patterns
3. **Analytics:** Calculate statistics and trends (all processed locally on your device)
4. **Data Export:** Generate PDF and CSV reports when you request them
5. **Widget Display:** Show medication information on your home screen widget

**We do not:**
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Sell or rent your data
- ❌ Use your data for advertising
- ❌ Send your data to remote servers
- ❌ Perform any server-side processing

---

## Third-Party Services

NorthStar does **NOT** use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics frameworks (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No cloud storage except Apple's iCloud (if you enable it)

---

## HealthKit Integration

### Permissions
The app requests permission to:
- **Read** sleep analysis data from HealthKit

### Usage
- HealthKit sleep data is used to automatically import sleep sessions
- You can grant or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → NorthStar
- Revoking access will not delete any data already imported

### Privacy
- We comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines
- HealthKit data is never shared with third parties
- HealthKit data is never used for advertising or marketing

---

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access Your Data
- View all your data within the app at any time
- Export your data as PDF or CSV files

### Delete Your Data
- Delete individual entries at any time
- Delete all app data by deleting the app
- Disable iCloud sync will not delete local data, only stop syncing

### Control Sync
- Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time
- Deleting data on one device will sync deletion to other devices (if iCloud enabled)

### Manage Permissions
- Control HealthKit access in iOS Settings
- Control notification permissions in iOS Settings

---

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- ✅ **Encryption:** All data encrypted using iOS Core Data encryption
- ✅ **Device Protection:** Data protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication
- ✅ **iCloud Encryption:** CloudKit data encrypted in transit and at rest
- ✅ **No Remote Storage:** No data stored on our servers
- ✅ **No Authentication:** No passwords or accounts that could be compromised

NorthStar is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed for personal health tracking by individuals managing their own health or by parents/guardians managing medications for their dependents.
